Our story is your upgrade.

Fynko started inside a tiny student apartment in Dublin. What began as a personal hunt for better tech accessories turned into a brand built on one promise: only sell what truly improves life.

2020 — The beginning

We were tired of overpriced, low-quality gadgets. So we started reviewing and curating the best ones.

2022 — The community grew

Our recommendations went viral among students, gamers and remote workers. Fynko became a trusted name.

2025 — A full curated store

Today we deliver premium essentials across Ireland, keeping the same mission: quality first, always.
